I am currently an undergraduate student in Zhejiang University of Technology, Jianxing honor college. I finished my high school in Fenghua High School, which is in Fenghua, Ningbo. After that, I came to Zhejiang University of Technology to start my undergraduate life. My GPA ranks first in the Electronic and Information Engineering department. Out of the love and interest in mathematics and physics, I actively participated in and won the Finalist of The Interdisciplinary Contest in Modeling. I spent a very unforgettable time in ZJUT and in the fall of 2021, I will start my PhD career in the Pervasive Computing Laboratory of Zhejiang University.

I especially like to use computers to solve practical problems and bring more convenience to people. I have done some research on interpretable AI and AI fairness, which are the two obstacles that currently plague the boom of AI. Now, I mainly focus on Human Activity Recognition(HAR). Specifically, it is to use better algorithms to recognize human gestures, which is of great significance especially for the elderly and the medical and health fields. I believe that now is the best age of computers, and I will strive to become a spray in the wave of the golden age of computers and contribute to the development of human wisdom.
